RATE LIMIT (lib/ArpCLI/RateLimit.pm, hooked in HTTP->request)
- Journal: ~/.cache/arpcli/usage (mode 0700 dir); tab-separated lines: epoch, key_fp, bucket
- Buckets: general (every request), server_create (POST /api/v1/servers), retry_after (429 cooldown deadline as until-epoch)
- Ceilings (10% margin): 54/min key, 108/min IP, 6/min server-create; 60s sliding window
- Flow: load journal on startup; acquire() before each HTTP call; mtime+flock resync if another arpcli wrote; prune expired; sleep() if at ceiling; append line
- 429 + Retry-After: record_retry_after() writes journal; wait() before retry; persists across runs
- Verbose: -v or ARPCLI_VERBOSE=1 warns before sleep; default silent
- Disable: ARPCLI_NO_RATE_LIMIT=1 or HARNESS_ACTIVE (prove)
